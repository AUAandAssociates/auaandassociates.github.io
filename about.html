Who We Are

A Legacy of Financial Excellence Across North India

AUA & Associates is a full-service Chartered Accountancy firm headquartered at Sector 20, Panchkula, Haryana with a branch office in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. We serve clients across Chandigarh Tricity and the Rohilkhand region.

Our team of qualified CAs combines technical expertise with deep local knowledge to deliver practical, compliant, and growth-oriented financial solutions across North India.

From Chandigarh Tricity to Bareilly Rohilkhand — we offer personalised CA service with the professionalism of a large firm and the personal touch of a boutique practice.

  • ICAI registered Chartered Accountancy firm
  • Serving clients across Chandigarh Tricity and Bareilly
  • Expert team with specialisations across all major CA domains
  • Digital-first, paperless processes for efficiency
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden charges
