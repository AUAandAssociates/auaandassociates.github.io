Who We Are

A Legacy of Financial Excellence in Panchkula

AUA & Associates is a full-service Chartered Accountancy firm headquartered at Sector 20, Panchkula, Haryana. We serve clients across Panchkula, Chandigarh, Mohali, Zirakpur, Ambala and the broader Tricity region.

Our team of qualified CAs and financial professionals combines technical expertise with deep local knowledge to deliver practical, compliant, and growth-oriented financial solutions for every business stage.

From a first-time GST registrant to an established enterprise seeking a Virtual CFO, we offer personalised service with the professionalism of a large firm and the personal touch of a boutique practice.

  • ICAI registered Chartered Accountancy firm
  • Serving our clients across Chandigarh Tricity
  • Expert team with specialisations across all major CA domains
  • Digital-first, paperless processes for efficiency
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden charges
