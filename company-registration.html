Frequently Asked

Company Registration FAQs

A Private Limited Company (Pvt Ltd) offers limited liability, easy fundraising ability, and better investor credibility. An LLP (Limited Liability Partnership) has lower compliance requirements and is preferred for professional services and smaller businesses. Our CAs advise you on the best structure for your goals.

A Private Limited Company can be registered in 7–10 working days, and an LLP in 5–7 working days, subject to MCA processing times. AUA & Associates handles the complete process including DSC, DIN, name reservation, and Certificate of Incorporation.

You need PAN card, Aadhaar, passport-size photo, and address proof for all directors; registered office address proof (utility bill + NOC from owner). AUA & Associates prepares all MCA forms and drafts the MOA/AOA for you.

After incorporation, companies must file annual returns (MGT-7), financial statements (AOC-4), hold board meetings, maintain statutory registers, conduct statutory audit, and file income tax returns. AUA & Associates offers annual compliance packages to handle all of this.

Yes. Company registration is an online process through the MCA portal and can be done for any location across India. We currently serve clients in Chandigarh, Panchkula, Bareilly, and Delhi NCR for all incorporation and compliance services.
