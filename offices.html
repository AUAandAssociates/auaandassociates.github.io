Office & Location FAQs

Common questions about visiting or contacting our offices.

Where is AUA & Associates office in Chandigarh? ▼
Our Chandigarh Tricity office is at GHS 111, A-605, Sector 20, Panchkula, Haryana 134117. It is easily accessible from Chandigarh, Mohali and Zirakpur. Call us at +91 6283 153 211 before visiting.
Where is AUA & Associates office in Bareilly? ▼
Our Bareilly office is at Khasra No. 705-708, Biharman Nagla, Pilibhit Bypass, near Phoenix United Mall, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh 243006. Contact: +91 8938 825 555.
What are the office hours? ▼
Both offices are open Monday to Saturday from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM. We are closed on Sundays, but WhatsApp is available for quick queries over the weekend.
Are all CA services available at both offices? ▼
Yes. GST filing, income tax, audit, company registration, financial advisory and Virtual CFO services are all available at both the Chandigarh Tricity and Bareilly offices.
Can I get CA services without visiting the office? ▼
Absolutely. AUA & Associates offers fully digital, paperless CA services. You can share documents via WhatsApp, email, or Google Drive and we will handle everything remotely — no office visit needed.
