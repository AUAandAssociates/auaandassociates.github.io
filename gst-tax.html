GST & Tax FAQs — Chandigarh Tricity

Who needs GST registration in Chandigarh / Panchkula? ▼
Any business with an annual turnover exceeding Contact for fee lakhs (Contact for fee lakhs for services) must register for GST. Businesses involved in inter-state supply, e-commerce, or import/export must register regardless of turnover. Our CA team can assess your specific situation.
What is the last date to file GST returns? ▼
GSTR-3B is due on the 20th of each month (monthly filers) or 22nd/24th (quarterly filers). GSTR-1 is due on the 11th monthly or 13th quarterly. We track all deadlines and file on time, every time.
Can AUA & Associates help with income tax notices? ▼
Yes, absolutely. We handle all types of income tax notices including scrutiny assessments, demand notices, and departmental proceedings. Our experienced CAs represent clients before income tax authorities in Chandigarh and Panchkula.
How do I claim maximum tax deductions? ▼
Our tax advisory service reviews your complete income profile and identifies all eligible deductions under sections 80C, 80D, 80G, HRA, LTA, home loan, etc. We also advise on tax-saving investments to minimise your liability legally.
