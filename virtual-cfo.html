What is a Virtual CFO?

CFO-Level Expertise, On-Demand

A Virtual CFO (vCFO) is a qualified financial expert who serves as your company's Chief Financial Officer on a part-time or retainer basis. You get all the strategic insight of a senior finance leader without the full-time salary, PF, and overhead costs.

AUA & Associates provides dedicated Virtual CFO services to growing businesses across the Chandigarh Tricity — helping you make smarter financial decisions, attract investors, and scale profitably.

  • Monthly MIS reports & financial dashboards
  • Cash flow planning and working capital management
  • Budgeting, forecasting & variance analysis
  • Investor pitch decks & fundraising support
  • Bank finance & CC/OD limit negotiations
  • Cost reduction & profitability improvement
  • Financial due diligence for M&A
  • Board-level financial reporting

FAQs

Virtual CFO — Common Questions

Which businesses need a Virtual CFO? ▼
Startups raising funds, SMEs with turnover of ₹1Cr+, businesses planning expansion, and companies preparing for audits or due diligence benefit greatly from Virtual CFO services. If you feel your financial reporting is reactive rather than strategic, it's time for a vCFO.
What is the cost of Virtual CFO services in Chandigarh? ▼
Our Virtual CFO retainers start at ₹15,000/month, depending on business size, complexity, and the hours required. We offer a free initial assessment before proposing any fee.
Can a Virtual CFO help with bank loans and fundraising? ▼
Absolutely. We prepare CMA data, project reports, financial models, and investor decks. We also assist in negotiations with banks for CC limits, term loans, and working capital facilities.
